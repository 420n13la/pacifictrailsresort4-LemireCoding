The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground Each yurt has canvas walls,a wooden flor and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished.

Each yurts is furnished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fired stove. Your luxury camping experience includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should i Bring

Most guests pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changng weather with light layers of clothing. Its also helpful to bring a flashlight and a sense of adventure!
